Whoami ?

Hello there, welcome to this showcase/bloggy website. I’m bWlrYQ but most people call me Mika, for understandable reasons right ? I’m a 20 years old French student that mainly studies networking and system administration (Windows/Linux) but that is when I’m not sleeping…

My studies aren’t really oriented towards cybersecurity for now but it still is my passion and I’ve been practicing for a couple of years now through platforms such as TryHackMe or Root-Me. I also often participate in CTF’s with the EGO+ team. On top of that I’m an active member of the Root-Me association and I am a volunteer in the support team since February 2022.

This website is for me a place where I can centralize Write-Ups from CTF’s, projects I made or even just post some cheatsheets related to IT security.
